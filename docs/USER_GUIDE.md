## 3. Crop Doctor

### What It Does

Upload a photo of a diseased plant and get an instant diagnosis with treatment recommendations.

### How to Use

1. Navigate to **ðŸŒ± Crop Doctor** from the sidebar
2. **Upload an image** of the affected plant (leaf, fruit, stem)
3. Optionally describe the symptoms in the text box
4. Click **"Diagnose"**
5. KrishiSaathi will:
   - Identify the disease
   - Explain the cause
   - Recommend treatment (organic + chemical options)
   - Estimate yield impact
   - Suggest prevention measures

### Tips for Best Photos

- Take photos in **natural daylight**
- Focus on the **affected area** (close-up)
- Include both **healthy and diseased** parts if possible
- Use the **rear camera** for better quality

### Supported Conditions

- Bacterial, fungal, and viral diseases
- Pest damage identification
- Nutrient deficiency symptoms (yellowing, wilting, curling)
- Over 150 crop diseases across major Indian crops

## 10. FAQ

### General

**Q: Is KrishiSaathi free?**
A: Yes, KrishiSaathi is completely free to use.

**Q: Do I need to install an app?**
A: No. KrishiSaathi runs in your web browser at the URL above.

**Q: Does it work on mobile?**
A: Yes. The interface is responsive and works on phones, tablets, and computers.

### Account

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link.

**Q: Can I change my language preference?**
A: Yes. Use the language dropdown in the sidebar (left panel).

### AI Responses

**Q: How accurate are the disease diagnoses?**
A: The AI uses a knowledge base of 150+ crop diseases and Gemini Vision for image analysis. While highly accurate, always verify critical diagnoses with a local agricultural extension officer.

**Q: Where does the market price data come from?**
A: From our curated database of APMC/mandi prices updated regularly, plus RAG-enhanced knowledge of market trends.

**Q: Does KrishiSaathi remember my previous conversations?**
A: Yes. Your chat history is saved and the AI remembers important details about your farm, crops, and location for personalized advice.

### Privacy

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. We use Supabase with Row-Level Security â€” only you can access your data. See our [Security documentation](SECURITY.md) for details.

**Q: Can other users see my conversations?**
A: No. Your chat history and memories are private and only accessible to you (and system admins for support purposes).
